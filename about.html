About St. Patrick Ventures

St. Patrick Ventures is a privately owned, multifaceted operated medical company specializing in Healthcare Consultations, Investments, Manufacturing and Specialty Medical Distribution. Its strategic marketing partnerships focusing on “unique exclusive” programs which focus on excellence. Specializing on providing a broad range of best in class medical consultants, medical devices, services, investments in products for variety of medical markets. We provide unparalleled customer service along with expertise. It enables and garners an impressive list of long term partnerships that hopes to continue thriving by fulfilling its founder’s vision of building a strong company focusing on the best practices in consultation, investments, manufacturing and specialty medical logistics. New ideas for products, new solutions for its customers, and new ways to improve patient health and safety. In other words, this creates a new era in healthcare. St. Patrick Ventures is excited about what the future holds, and is looking forward to making you a part of the future St. Patrick Ventures story.

St. Patrick Ventures Founders

Is a privately owned and operated company founded by Sue Morrimotto (CFO), Mr. Daniel Burns ( President) and Mr. Charles Cresine (investor) in 2014 founded by investing in a company called St. Patrick Medical. However, in 2016 it evolved into St. Patrick Ventures by providing a broad range of best in class medical disposable products, services, investments and patient care items for all medical markets. It is proud of its reputation for attracting and developing some of the brightest leaders in the medical technology industry. The strong leadership is vital to achieving the goals and fulfilling St. Patrick Ventures. Its executive leadership team members serve as role models. Not only do they provide strategic oversight and motivation, they also contribute to the collective good of the company and the industry—by serving on Inclusion Councils, leading volunteer efforts in its communities, and collaborating with industry peers to improve healthcare policies. Its products are produced through strategic marketing partnerships with a focus on excellence. The company’s focus to unparalleled customer service that which enables St. Patrick Healthcare: Consultation, Investments, Manufacturing & Specialty Medical Distribution to stay ahead of the competition. By offering customers a variety of ways to streamline its costs and improve efficiencies. It has garnered an impressive list of long term partnerships. St. Patrick Ventures continues to thrive by fulfilling its founder’s vision, “to build a global company focused on the best practices in consultancy, investments, manufacturing and logistics.”
